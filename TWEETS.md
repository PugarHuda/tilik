**2/ (the core)**
For each pack, Tilik checks Renaiss' *own* stated EV against the last 30 real pulls.

OMEGA: stated $51.84 → observed mean $48.83. Consistent ✅

And it shows the 95% confidence interval, because it's a small sample and I won't hide the noise.

**3/ (honesty)**
It's honest about skew, too.

Eden's *average* pull beats the rip — but 80% of pulls still lose. So it's not a green "+EV."
It's labeled **"+EV but top-heavy."**

The average alone flatters the odds. A good tool says so.

**4/ (the wedge)**
Here's the part Renaiss can't credibly ship itself: every FMV is Renaiss valuing Renaiss.

So Tilik cross-checks it against an *independent*, sale-based estimate from the Renaiss OS Index.

One card: Renaiss FMV $40 · independent est. $16.62. I just flag it — you decide.

**5/ (verifiable)**
It's all verifiable. Each card links its real BNB Chain transfers to BscScan — that's what
"verifiable on-chain" actually looks like.

Paste any PSA cert (or a photo of the slab 📸) for an independent valuation on the spot.

And the fun one: "Should I rip?" 🎰

Monte Carlo, 5,000 runs. Rip OMEGA 10× → here's your expected P&L, your chance of profit, and the
full distribution of outcomes.
